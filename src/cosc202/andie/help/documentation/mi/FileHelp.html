Kōnae

He aha te Mahi a ia Mahi i te Whaiaro Kōnae:

Whakatuwheratia Whakatuwheratia he whakaahua Ki te whakatūwherahia i mua i te whakatika me te tiaki ki ANDIE, ka whai mai ngā whakatika Pūtātara: ctrl + o

Tiaki Tiaki he whakaahua me ngā mahi whakaahua kua whakatuwheratia i ngā kōnae whakawhitiwhiti Pūtātara: ctrl + s

Tiaki he Tāruarua Tiaki he whakaahua me ngā mahi whakaahua kua whakatuwheratia i ngā kōnae whakawhitiwhiti ki tētahi wāhi/ingoa hōu Pūtātara: ctrl + shift + s

Tuku Tāruarua Tiaki i te wāhanga whakatika o tētahi whakaahua ki tētahi wāhi whakarāpopototanga whakarārangiā
