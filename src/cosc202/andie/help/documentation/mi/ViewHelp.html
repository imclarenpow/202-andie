Tirohia

Te Mahi a ia Pātene i te Tirohanga Whārangi:

Whakakōkiri: Whakakōkirihia te whakararuraru a 10%

Whakakī: Whakakīhia te whakararuraru a 10%

Whakakōkirihia ki te Rerekētanga o te Matapihi: Whakakōkirihia/whakakīhia te whakararuraru o te whakaahua kia whiwhi i te rahi o te matapihi o ANDIE

Whakakītina: Whakatika te whakararuraru ki te rahi tūturu o te whakaahua

Reo: Whakaputahia te whakamahi e whakarerekē i te reo o ANDIE. Ko ngā reo e wātea ana i tēnei wā ko te Reo Pākehā, te Reo Hapanīhi me te Reo Māori

Whakakōritanga Matapihi ki te Rahi Whakaahua: Whakakōrikorihia te matapihi o ANDIE ki te rahi o te whakaahua o nāianei
